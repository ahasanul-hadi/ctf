Rule of Engagement

  1. All Financial Institution & Critical Information Infrastructure (CII) shall register in our platform using the registration guideline (https://www.cirt.gov.bd/financial-cii-cyber-drill-2023/) and https://cyberdrill.cirt.gov.bd/reg-guide.
  2. Each organization register one team containing 3 to 5 members.
  3. Registration fee is 10,000 (Ten Thousand only) Taka per team.
  4. All members can submit the flag and be carful about limited submission attempt.
  5. All communication conducted through this particular email address (cyberrange@cirt.gov.bd). You are requested to allow email communication from this email address for the test date and drill date. We are also using Discord as instant messaging during drill day for general discussion. Please follow the naming convention in Discord channel. Naming convention in discord: “TeamName_PersonName”.
  6. It is not allowed to engage people from outside of the organization.
  7. Participants have the freedom to use any tool including those available on the internet.
  8. Any sort of Denial of Service or any other unwanted action against the servers or associated infrastructures are prohibited.
  9. Do not perform any unwanted actions against other teams.
  10. Do not try to exchange flags/write-ups/hints of the challenges during the competition with other teams participating or other external entities.
  11. Violation of organizer’s terms will terminate the team from the competition.
  12. Top 30 teams will participate in the on-premises cyber drill, which will held in MIST on 21 October 2023. Top 3 teams will be provided crest & prizes.
  13. All right reserved to BGD e-GOV CIRT for any changes.
